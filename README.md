## 📊 Featured Capstone Project
### Food Delivery Ecosystem & Customer Behavior Analytics

* **Workbook File:** `CAPSTONE.xlsx`
* **Objective:** Cleaned, structured, and analyzed a regional food delivery dataset to evaluate restaurant performance, geographic demand clusters, and payment preferences.

#### 🗂️ Workbook Architecture
The analysis is seamlessly managed across three core environments within **CAPSTONE.xlsx**:
*   **`RAW DATA`**: The central repository containing 500 detailed transactional records tracking metrics like revenue, quantity, location, customer age groups, and churn status.
*   **`PROJECT TASK`**: The analytical workbench where complex cross-tabulations, data validations, and pivot calculations were executed.
*   **`DASHBOARD`**: An executive control center that aggregates macro-KPIs into a high-level visual breakdown for stakeholders.

#### 📈 Key Insights & Achievements
*   **Financial Scope:** Managed and visualized data representing **$1,199,575.98 in Total Revenue** across **500 total orders** and **1,480 units sold**.
*   **Market Dominance:** Identified **Pizza Hut** as the top revenue driver (**$265,411.62**), closely followed by Burger King and KFC.
*   **Geographic & Menu Demand:** Uncovered that **Multan** recorded the highest operational order volume, while **Continental dishes** led sales quantities platform-wide.
*   **Consumer Insights:** Determined that the vast majority of consumers prefer **card payments** over cash or mobile wallets. 
*   **Quality Baseline:** Maintained an **Average Customer Rating of 3.08 out of 5**, mapping out clear data pathways to optimize fulfillment and address order cancellations.
